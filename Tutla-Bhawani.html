Tutla Bhawani Waterfall







Tutla Bhawani also known as Tutrahi and Tutla Dham is one of the beautiful waterfalls in the Rohtas district of Bihar. It has a wonderful environment with a beautiful waterfall covered by two mountain ranges which makes the valley the most beautiful tourist places in Bihar. It is located nearly to Tilauthu and approx 20 km southwest of Dehri-On-Sone. Near the waterfall, there are two mountains from north-west and south-east.

The two large mountains one from the north-west and another from the south-east converge to create a green valley which stretches to 1 mile. Once you reach Dehri-On-Sone one of our representatives will pick you and will take you to the Tutla Bhawani Waterfall. You can enjoy your day outing in nature with the cool breeze of Tutla Bhawani waterfall. One can also visit the Tutla Bhawani Temple which is nearby the waterfall. All this creates a fascinating scene, from east this valley stretches 300 M wick wharves, from west it shrinks to only 50M from west a spring fall is created which falls from a height of 200 M.

Starting point: Dehri-On-Sone Ram Dehra, Bihar Trip duration: 3 hours
How to reach? The location of the activity is in Dehri-On-Sone Ram Dehra. The exact location will be shared with you after booking. You may reach the meeting spot on your personal vehicle or you may opt for Pickup & Drop at extra cost.
